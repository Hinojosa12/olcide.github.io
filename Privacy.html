Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

CaribZoom Inc ("we", "us", or "our") operates the CaribZoom Staff Dashboard and manages Facebook Pages for our family of brands. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services, including messages sent through Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and other social media platforms.

1. Information We Collect

When you send a message to any of our brand pages on Facebook, Instagram, or other platforms, we may collect:

  • Your name and profile information as provided by the platform
  • The content of your messages
  • Timestamps and metadata associated with your messages
  • Your platform user ID

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Manage and improve our customer service operations
  • Centralize communications across our brands for efficient service
  • Monitor and improve the quality of our responses

3. Our Brands

CaribZoom Inc manages the following brands, each with their own Facebook Page and social media presence:

  • Destiny's Clothing Store
  • Pieces, Plus Sized - Reloaded
  • D'Jango Gentleman's Apparel
  • Hope Jewellery
  • The Office Depot
  • Home Essentials
  • Party Hub

4. Data Storage and Security

Your messages and associated information are stored securely using industry-standard practices. We use encrypted connections and restrict access to authorized personnel only. Message data is stored in secure cloud services with appropriate access controls.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our dashboard (e.g., hosting providers)
  • When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

6. Data Retention

We retain your message data for as long as necessary to provide customer support and for our legitimate business purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • File a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

8. Facebook Platform Data

Our use of information received from Facebook APIs adheres to the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies, including the limited use requirements.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
